# Interview with Grani — September 2024, Session 1

INTERVIEWER: Tell me about the day the factory burned down.

GRANI: Well, I remember it clear as anything. It was a Tuesday, hot as
blazes, and Pa came running up the road hollering that the mill was on
fire. We could see the smoke from the porch.

INTERVIEWER: How old were you?

GRANI: I was eight years old. My little brother Thomas was still in
diapers.
